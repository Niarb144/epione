    About Us

    From Africa for Africa's health market

    • Empowering Young Mothers: Training on Safe Home Remedies for Family Health

    As a pharmaceutical wholesaler and distributor, our business provides medicines and healthcare products to pharmacies, hospitals, and other healthcare providers

    In doing so, Epione plays a crucial role in the pharmaceutical supply chain, bridging the gap between manufacturers and end-users. We manage the storage, distribution, and logistics of pharmaceutical products, ensuring that medicines are available when and where they are needed.

    Epione Health and SDG 3: Advancing Good Health and Well-being

    • Ensuring Access to Essential Medicines
    • Providing Sanitary and Maternity Pads for Underserved Populations
    • Promoting Preventive and Community-based Health Solutions

    Ensuring Access to Essential Medicines

    We support equitable healthcare by sourcing and distributing high-quality, affordable essential medicines. Our goal is to reduce the burden of disease and ensure that no one is left behind due to lack of access to life-saving treatments.

    Providing Sanitary and Maternity Pads for Underserved Populations

    Recognizing that menstrual and maternal health are critical yet often overlooked components of well-being, we supply biodegradable sanitary pads and maternity pads to girls and women in rural and underserved communities. These products not only improve hygiene and dignity but also support school attendance and safe motherhood.

    Promoting Preventive and Community-based Health Solutions

    Beyond products, we champion awareness and education initiatives focused on hygiene, reproductive health, and community wellness, empowering people with knowledge to lead healthier lives.
