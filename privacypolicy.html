Last Updated: October 13th, 2025

We may collect the following information when you use our contact form:

  • Your organization (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to respond to inquiries, improve our services, and communicate with you when necessary.

If you have any privacy concerns, please reach out at info@epione-health.com.
